🏗️ WHAT IS THIS PACKAGE?

This package holds our **core business logic, clients, and GraphQL machinery** related to Saleor:
- Saleor Codegen outputs
- URQL clients
- Reusable queries/mutations
- Utility fns for pricing, stock, availability, etc.

Saleor is *not just a backend*, it's our infrastructure backbone.
This package is the glue between Saleor and our apps.

---

WHY THE NAME?

We wanted a name that:
- Combines **"core"** (central logic)
- With **"commerce"** (Saleor infra)
- But isn’t as boring as `core-commerce`

We chose **"coremmerce"** — a portmanteau of core + commerce.
It’s clean. It’s cursed. It’s confusing *just enough*.
